How to install on Google TV / Android TV

  • Open this page on your TV browser, or download on phone/PC and transfer the APK.
  • Enable app installs from unknown sources on your device.
  • Install app-debug.apk.
  • Open the app, set your server URL (example: http://192.168.1.100:3000).
  • Use the same Wi‑Fi network for your uploader device and TV.
